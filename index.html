Artisan Creations

Welcome to Mud Made, where artistry and craftsmanship blend to create unique, handmade pieces for your home. At Mud Made, we believe in the beauty of individuality and the charm of artisanal creations.

Our mission is to bring a touch of uniqueness to your kitchen, living spaces, and beyond through our carefully crafted kitchenware, home decor, and engaging workshops. Every Mud Made piece begins with a lump of clay and a vision. Our skilled artisans shape and mold each item by hand, ensuring that no two pieces are exactly alike. We take pride in using sustainable materials and eco-friendly practices throughout our process, from sourcing our clay to the final firing in our kilns.

Shipping FAQs

Do you offer shipping?

+

Yes, we offer shipping for all our pottery items.

How much does shipping cost?

+

Shipping costs vary based on the size and weight of your order and your location. You can get an estimate during checkout.

Do you offer international shipping?

+

Yes, we ship internationally to most countries.

How long will it take to receive my order?

+

Domestic orders typically arrive within 5 business days after processing. International orders may take longer due to customs.

Can I track my order?

+

Yes, once shipped, we provide a tracking number for you to monitor your shipment's progress.
